Biography

Kotaro Abe is a Japanese creative technologist with two faces; Tech-enthusiast who loves to create interesting communications/ expressions, as well as Experience specialist who focuses on both on physical and screen based interactions.

Kotaro's design projects often deal with current social issues and aim to question / activate our mindset toward them. In order to show one of the plausible ways to tackle the issues, Kotaro utilises his inter-disciplinary skills; prototyping, electric engineering, software coding, visual design and film making. His works have been globally exhibited in Sonar Festival, Ars Electronica Festival, NTT ICC Open Space, ACM SIGGRAPH and Laval Virtual.
