SPIN


This object named SPIN (Social Project for Instrumental Notation) is a social object for
1.enhancing the social aspect of the domestic household
2.turning communal spaces into social surface
3. combining social, spatial, and musical spheres
4. most importantly bringing people together to enhance and augment their experience.
